Two Month Break

Taking a two month break isn't the greatest thing to do. I can't say that it's helped anything here. But when you don't have the mental bandwidth, you don't have the mental bandwidth.

But, being on vacation and getting extra sleep and exercise makes things easier. A 4km walk in the mornings is a good thing. Spending time with my teenaged children (and them wanting to spend time with me!) is a good thing. It's given me some energy. Just a little bit, but enough that I am willing to spend it on This Silly Project. So back to it, then.

(Yes, I realize it's been more than two months since I've written here. This blog isn't my focus, though maybe it wouldn't hurt to put a little more into it.)
