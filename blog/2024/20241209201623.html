Monday, December 9th, 2024 - 20:16

If I call it middle of January to take the Christmastime into account (because I want to spend as much time with my adult children while they're home as I can, of course!), I think I can make it.

But you know what? I'm not putting a timeline on this, and I'm not forcing myself. I'm doing this because I want to enjoy myself, dangit, not because I have a deadline.

Anyhoo, catch ya soon.
